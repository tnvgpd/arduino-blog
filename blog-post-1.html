Welcome to My Engineering Journey!

Published on April 25, 2026

Hello! Welcome to Tanvi’s Engineering Journey! Or… welcome to my first post!

The most intimidating part of learning is starting. While getting into the rhythm is difficult, once you are in, it is quite hard to stop. So, here is my attempt to overcome that challenge and start.

I recently started my endeavor with an Arduino UNO Q board, a set of 37 sensors, antigravity, and a whole load of parts my father had from his creating days. While trying various projects, I began to wonder how I could keep track of my progress.

What better way to document my journey, than to start a blog! That's why I made this page. I intend to show my creations, from small to big, and inspire more youth creators.

So here is a recap on all my materials:

  • Arduino UNO Q
  • Elegoo 37 Sensor Kit v2.0
  • Sanguinololu (ATmega644P)
  • Google Antigravity Extension
  • Numerous other parts I yet to sift through!

Using antigravity, I explained my situation: my level of understanding, my experience, my resources, and my intended goals. Then it designed a 6 Level lesson plan. This lesson plan guides me through the basics, like sensors, into the complex topics, like using Sanguinololu (which I am currently struggling to spell).

So, thank you for joining me on my first blog post! I will be updating this blog regularly as I start new projects, struggle with failed deployments, and experiment with new parts. Make sure to visit this blog regularly to hear about updates on my experiments! Can’t wait to see you next time!
